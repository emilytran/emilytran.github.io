What is the Enneagram?
The Enneagram is one of many tools for self-discovery, awareness, and personal growth.

It's based on the premise that you developed your personality as a coping mechanism to your childhood messaging and describes why you think, feel and behave in particular ways based upon core fears and core desires.

Two people can be seen doing same actions but for different underlying motivations. Being aware of these is the first step of deconditioning, letting go of self-limiting beliefs, and shaping who you want to be.

There are free tests such as on Your Enneagram Coach or you can reflect on the types on your own.
